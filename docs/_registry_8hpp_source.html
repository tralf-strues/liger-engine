40  * @brief Registry of assets contained in an asset folder.
41  *
42  * Manages mapping from asset uuids to their physical file paths.
43  * All this information is also gets saved to the corresponding registry file.
44  *
45  * Example structure of an asset folder:
46  * @code{.unparsed}
47  * assets/
48  * .lregistry
49  * textures/
50  * player/
51  * player_albedo.png
52  * player_normal.png
53  * materials/
54  * player.lmat
55  * meshes/
56  * player.lmesh
57  * scenes/
58  * scene0.lscene
59  * sounds/
60  * player_hello.mp3
61  * player_goodbye.mp3
62  * @endcode
63  *
64  * Example contents of the corresponding `.liger-registry` file:
65  * @code{.unparsed}
66  * - file: textures/player/player_albedo.png
67  * id: 0x7449545984958451
68  * - file: textures/player/player_normal.png
69  * id: 0x2435204985724523
70  * - file: materials/player.lmat
71  * id: 0x9208347234895237
72  * - file: meshes/player.lmesh
73  * id: 0x9045734534058964
74  * - file: scenes/scene0.lscene
75  * id: 0x1894576549867059
76  * - file: sounds/player_hello.mp3
77  * id: 0x5924984576345097
78  * - file: sounds/player_goodbye.mp3
79  * id: 0x2489524375902435
80  * @endcode
81  */
